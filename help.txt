Usage:
  ./ltranslate --start <target_lang> <audio_source>

Description:
  LTranslate captures English audio from a desktop audio source,
  transcribes it in real time, and translates it to the target language.

Arguments:
  <target_lang>
      Target language code for translation.
      Examples: pt, es, fr, de, ja, zh

  <audio_source>
      Desktop audio source/monitor name.
      This is usually a PulseAudio or PipeWire monitor device.
      Example:
        alsa_output.usb-0c76_PH259-00.iec958-stereo.monitor
